üîß Integration Instructions

For Offer Networks:

Configure your postback URL as:

https://yourdomain.com/postback?appid={app_id}&userid={user_id}&payout={payout}

Replace the placeholders with actual values:

  • {app_id} - Your application/offer ID
  • {user_id} - The user's unique identifier
  • {payout} - The payout amount in USD
